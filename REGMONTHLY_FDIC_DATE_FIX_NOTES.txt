RegMonthly FDIC Date Fix Notes
Date: 2026-05-11

Purpose
- Fix wrong published_at dates showing on FDIC press release articles.
- Keep the change isolated to FDIC only.

What changed
- scripts/build.py now confirms FDIC article dates from the FDIC detail page instead of relying on the listing date.
- Added an FDIC-only detail parser that prioritizes:
  1. the written article date near the FDIC article title/body,
  2. the FDIC "Last Updated" date,
  3. FDIC metadata as a final fallback.
- Other source date parsing logic was not changed.

Why
- FDIC pages can expose unrelated template/listing/sidebar dates before the real article date.
- The prior generic parsing could grab those template dates and display the wrong date on the monthly site and smart JSON.

Files changed
- scripts/build.py

After applying
- Re-run the monthly build when ready.
- Confirm FDIC examples show their real article date in docs/raw/items-array.json and docs/raw/items-smart-100.json.
